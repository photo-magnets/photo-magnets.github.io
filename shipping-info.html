Shipping Info

Shipping guidance for photo magnets, custom photo magnets, fridge magnets, and memory magnet gift sets.

Production and Delivery

Production timing depends on the print provider you choose from the Shop. Custom designs usually require image review, print production, and carrier transit.

For gift deadlines, order custom photo magnets early and choose tracked shipping where available.

Packing Expectations

Good providers ship photo magnets flat or protected in rigid packaging so corners, surfaces, and magnetic backing arrive clean.
